Barda Hills

Hardly 15 km from the coast of Porbandar town of Porbandar district, the hilly forest of Barda is stands against the salinity ingress in the region. This area was declared as sanctuary in February '1979.

Dwarka

Nestled on the western coast of India, overlooking the azure Arabian Sea, lies the ancient city of Dwarka. Renowned for its rich cultural heritage and profound spiritual significance, Dwarka is a captivating destination that draws pilgrims and tourists alike from across the globe.

Ghumli

Ghumli is a village located 45 km from Porbandar, at foothills of Barda, in Devbhumi Dwarka district of Gujarat, India and It is archeologist site.

Bhanvad

Bhanvad is a city and a municipality in Devbhoomi Dwarka district in the state of Gujarat, India. Bhanvad is named after Jethwa ruler Rana Bhanji Jetva of Ghumli, who founded this place.

Somnath Temple

Nestled along the serene shores of the Arabian Sea in the Prabhas Patan near Veraval in Saurashtra, Gujarat, the Somnath Temple stands as a testament to the timeless spirituality and cultural heritage of India.
